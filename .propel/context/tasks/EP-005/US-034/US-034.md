# US-034: Enforce 24-Hour Reconciliation SLA with Escalation

> **Epic:** EP-005 — Medication Reconciliation Agent | **Sprint:** 2 | **Points:** 3 | **Priority:** Must Have
> **Status:** Draft | **Date:** 2026-07-14

---

## User Story

**As a** charge pharmacist,
**I want to** be automatically notified when a medication reconciliation has not been completed within 24 hours of admission,
**So that** I can manually prioritise the reconciliation before the patient is discharged with unreviewed medications.

---

## Business Value

CMS Conditions of Participation require medication reconciliation within 24 hours of admission (BR-002). Missing this deadline is a regulatory violation that can result in hospital survey deficiencies. Automated escalation ensures no reconciliation falls through the cracks due to workload peaks.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

### Scenario 1: 24-hour SLA breach triggers charge pharmacist escalation
**Given** an A01 admission event occurred 24 hours ago and the `MEDICATION_RECONCILIATION` `AgentTask` is still not `COMPLETED`
**When** the SLA monitor runs
**Then** a `CHARGE_PHARMACIST_ESCALATION` notification is published to `notification-requests` with `encounter_id`, `patient_unit`, and `hours_elapsed=24`.

### Scenario 2: Completed reconciliation does not trigger escalation
**Given** a `MEDICATION_RECONCILIATION` AgentTask with `status=COMPLETED` and `completed_at` within 24 hours of admission
**When** the SLA monitor runs
**Then** no escalation is fired for that encounter.

### Scenario 3: Escalation is not repeated every monitor cycle
**Given** an escalation was already sent for encounter `ENC-001`
**When** the SLA monitor runs again 5 minutes later
**Then** no duplicate escalation is published (idempotency ensured via `sla_escalation_sent_at` field on `AgentTask`).

### Scenario 4: Manual override clears SLA breach flag
**Given** a charge pharmacist manually marks a reconciliation as `REVIEWED_MANUALLY` via the API
**When** `PATCH /api/v1/encounters/{id}/tasks/{task_id}/override` is called
**Then** `AgentTask.sla_escalation_sent_at` is cleared; `AgentTask.status=COMPLETED`; no further escalations fire for this task.

---

## Definition of Done

- [ ] SLA monitor checks `MEDICATION_RECONCILIATION` tasks: escalate if `IN_PROGRESS` or `PENDING` >24 hours from `encounter.admit_time`
- [ ] `sla_escalation_sent_at` nullable timestamp on `agent_task` (prevents duplicate escalation)
- [ ] Escalation published to `notification-requests` Pub/Sub with `priority=HIGH`
- [ ] `PATCH /api/v1/encounters/{id}/tasks/{task_id}/override` endpoint for manual completion
- [ ] Unit tests: escalation at 24h, no duplicate escalation, completed task no escalation, override
- [ ] Code reviewed and approved

---

## Technical Notes

- SLA monitor is the same APScheduler instance as US-021 (coordinator SLA) — add a medication-specific check to the same scheduler
- `admit_time` sourced from `encounter.admit_time` (set by the A01 event handler)
- Override endpoint RBAC: only `charge_pharmacist` or `pharmacy_supervisor` role may override
