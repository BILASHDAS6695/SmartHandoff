# US-005: Escalate to Charge Pharmacist if Reconciliation Not Completed Within 24 Hours

> **Epic:** EP-005 — Medication Reconciliation Agent | **Sprint:** 2 | **Points:** 2 | **Priority:** Must Have

## User Story

**As a** charge pharmacist,
**I want** an automatic escalation if medication reconciliation is not completed within 24 hours of admission,
**so that** CMS requirements (BR-002) are met and no patient goes without a completed medication review.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] **AC-1:** MedRecon AgentTask has a separate SLA of 24 hours (overriding the default 30-minute SLA in EP-003 US-002); configurable via Secret Manager env var `MEDRECON_SLA_HOURS=24`
- [ ] **AC-2:** At 24-hour mark with reconciliation incomplete: escalation notification sent to charge pharmacist (role=PHARMACIST with `is_charge=true`) via SMS + dashboard SignalR alert
- [ ] **AC-3:** `AgentTask.status` updated to `ESCALATED` at 24-hour mark; escalation includes: patient encounter ID, admission time, elapsed time, and a link to the reconciliation queue
- [ ] **AC-4:** Escalation dashboard alert uses RED severity badge; charge pharmacist can acknowledge via `PATCH /api/v1/alerts/{id}/acknowledge`
- [ ] **AC-5:** Reconciliation completion resets the escalation: if pharmacist completes after escalation, `AgentTask.status=COMPLETE` and escalation alert auto-dismissed

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Integration test: create AgentTask with start_time 25 hours ago → verify charge pharmacist receives escalation notification
- [ ] Escalation cleared when reconciliation marked complete (tested in staging)

## Dependencies

- EP-003 US-002 (SLA escalation framework)
- EP-013 US-001 (SMS notification for charge pharmacist)
