# US-003: Human Escalation from Chatbot to On-Call Nurse Within 2 Minutes

> **Epic:** EP-008 — Patient Communication Agent & Chatbot | **Sprint:** 2 | **Points:** 3 | **Priority:** Must Have

## User Story

**As a** patient,
**I want** to connect to a real nurse from the chatbot when the AI can't answer my question,
**so that** I always have a human fallback for concerns that are beyond what a chatbot can handle.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] **AC-1:** Chatbot response includes a "Connect with Care Team" button after every AI response; patient can tap at any time to request human escalation
- [ ] **AC-2:** `POST /api/v1/chat/escalate` → publishes `PATIENT_ESCALATION_REQUEST` to `notification-requests`; Notification Service alerts on-call nurse via SMS and SignalR dashboard push
- [ ] **AC-3:** Patient sees: "Your nurse has been notified. Expected response within 2 minutes." with a countdown timer; nurse name shown when they acknowledge the escalation
- [ ] **AC-4:** Nurse acknowledges via `PATCH /api/v1/chat/escalation/{id}/acknowledge`; patient portal updates to show nurse name and confirmation message
- [ ] **AC-5:** Unacknowledged escalation after 5 minutes escalates to charge nurse; after 10 minutes → clinical director; escalation chain and timing logged in `alert_log`
- [ ] **AC-6:** Integration test: patient taps "Connect with Care Team" → nurse SMS received within 60 seconds → nurse acknowledges within 2 minutes → patient portal shows confirmation

## Definition of Done

- [ ] End-to-end escalation chain tested in staging (patient → nurse → acknowledgement → patient confirmation)
- [ ] 5-minute escalation to charge nurse verified with synthetic time advance

## Dependencies

- EP-008 US-001 (Chatbot framework)
- EP-013 US-001 (SMS notification to nurse)
- EP-003 US-003 (SignalR push for nurse dashboard alert)
