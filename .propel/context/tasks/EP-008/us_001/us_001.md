# US-001: 24/7 AI Chatbot Scoped to Patient's Own Discharge Instructions

> **Epic:** EP-008 — Patient Communication Agent & Chatbot | **Sprint:** 2 | **Points:** 8 | **Priority:** Must Have

## User Story

**As a** discharged patient,
**I want** a 24/7 AI chatbot in my portal that answers questions about my own discharge instructions,
**so that** I can get answers at 2am when no one is available — without calling the ED.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] **AC-1:** `POST /api/v1/chat` accepts patient JWT, message text, and encounter_id; assembles context: system prompt (2K tokens) + patient discharge summary (4K max) + conversation history (2K max, FIFO-pruned to last 10 messages)
- [ ] **AC-2:** Vertex AI Gemini Flash called with assembled context; response streamed to patient within 3 seconds for 95% of standard queries (not urgency/escalation)
- [ ] **AC-3:** Response scope enforced: system prompt instructs agent to answer only from the provided patient documents; questions about other patients or general medical advice are deflected with "Please consult your care team for general medical questions"
- [ ] **AC-4:** Conversation history stored in Redis key `conversation-history:{encounter_id}` (last 10 messages); persisted to encrypted `chatbot_transcript` table
- [ ] **AC-5:** Chatbot activated on `DISCHARGE_COMPLETE` Pub/Sub event; remains active for 30 days post-discharge; after 30 days returns "Your post-discharge support period has ended — please contact your care team"
- [ ] **AC-6:** Context window capped at 8K total tokens; conversation history pruned FIFO when exceeding 2K token allocation

## Definition of Done

- [ ] 3-second response SLA verified for 95% of 50 test queries in staging with real Gemini Flash calls
- [ ] Scope enforcement tested: query about another patient → correctly deflected
- [ ] 30-day expiry tested with synthetic date offset

## Dependencies

- EP-004 US-001 (Finalised discharge documents)
- EP-010 US-001 (Patient portal for chatbot UI)
- EP-TECH US-001 (Redis for conversation history)
