# US-003: Schedule 48-Hour Post-Discharge Check-In for At-Risk Patients

> **Epic:** EP-007 — Follow-up Care Agent & Risk Scoring | **Sprint:** 2 | **Points:** 2 | **Priority:** Should Have

## User Story

**As a** patient with a risk score ≥0.5,
**I want** a check-in message from my care team 48 hours after discharge,
**so that** I know someone is monitoring my recovery and I have a prompt to report any concerns.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] **AC-1:** On risk score ≥0.5 at discharge: Follow-up Care Agent schedules a `CHECK_IN` notification in `notification-requests` Pub/Sub with `send_at = discharge_time + 48 hours`
- [ ] **AC-2:** Notification Service delivers check-in SMS/email 48 hours post-discharge: message includes patient's first name, care team contact number, and a link to the patient portal chatbot
- [ ] **AC-3:** Check-in message delivery tracked in `notification_log`; undeliverable check-ins escalate to assigned nurse (FR-053) for manual outreach
- [ ] **AC-4:** Check-in messages configurable per hospital: template, wording, and contact number stored in Secret Manager per `hospital_id`
- [ ] **AC-5:** Integration test: create discharge with risk_score=0.6 → verify notification scheduled for 48h → simulate time advance → verify notification dispatched

## Definition of Done

- [ ] 48-hour check-in verified in staging with synthetic 48-hour time offset
- [ ] Undeliverable escalation tested: failed SMS → nurse alerted within 15 minutes

## Dependencies

- EP-007 US-001 (Risk score calculated)
- EP-013 US-001 (Notification Service scheduled dispatch)
