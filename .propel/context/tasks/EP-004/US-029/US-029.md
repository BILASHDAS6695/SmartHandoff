## User Story

**As a** Compliance Officer,
**I want to** ensure all AI-generated documents display a prominent "AI-Assisted â€” Review Required" label until a qualified clinician explicitly approves them,
**So that** care team members always know the provenance of clinical content and no AI-generated text is acted upon as finalised without human review.

---

## Business Value

HIPAA and Joint Commission standards require clear labelling of computer-assisted clinical documentation. The AI-Assisted label (BR-011) is a patient safety guardrail that prevents clinicians from accidentally treating a draft AI summary as a finalised clinical document. Clinician approval gating (BR-001) creates a mandatory human-in-the-loop review step.

## Acceptance Criteria

### Scenario 1: AI-Assisted label shown on all generated documents in review UI
**Given** a document with `ai_assisted_label=True` and `status=PENDING_REVIEW`
**When** a care team member views the document in the review UI
**Then** a prominent yellow banner "âš  AI-Assisted â€” Review Required" is displayed at the top of both document panes.

### Scenario 2: Label removed after clinician approval
**Given** a physician has clicked "Approve" and `Document.status=APPROVED`
**When** the approved document is viewed in the patient portal or review UI
**Then** the "AI-Assisted" banner is absent; the document shows "Approved by [physician_name] on [date]" footer.

### Scenario 3: Document cannot be sent to patient portal before approval
**Given** a document with `status=PENDING_REVIEW`
**When** `GET /api/v1/portal/documents?encounter_id={id}` is called
**Then** the response excludes all `PENDING_REVIEW` documents; only `APPROVED` documents are returned to the patient portal.

### Scenario 4: Approval sets audit fields
**Given** Dr. David calls `PATCH /api/v1/documents/{id}/approve`
**When** the approval is processed
**Then** `Document.approved_at` is set to current UTC timestamp; `Document.reviewed_by_user_id` is set to Dr. David's user ID; `Document.ai_assisted_label` remains `True` (label history preserved); `Document.status=APPROVED`.
